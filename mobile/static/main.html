THE

KITCHEN

ARCHITECTURE 

BEGAN WITH A DREAM TO TRANSFORM LIVING SPACES INTO A WORD OF ART.

Architects worldwide understand the profound impact that the quality of a space has on life and strive to enhance it. True quality, however, is something that can only be created when it has been experienced, seen, or felt.This is what we have cultivated over years of collaboration with discerning clients, renowned global brands, and perfectionist architects. These experiences have taught us how to bring tangible, heartfelt quality to life in every design and execution.Bocaveli’s architects, who have worked extensively with top global brands such as Lualdi and Poggenpohl, deeply understand and embody this level of quality. This profound awareness shines through in every design and project we undertake.Each project at Bocaveli is crafted through the synergy of creative architects and skilled artisans, working with passion and precision to create something truly unique.We believe that great design is not only beautiful but also evokes emotions and enriches life.Bocaveli can be likened to a bonsai tree—small in size yet mature, refined, and deeply valuable. Like a bonsai, every detail is meticulously cultivated, embodying both artistry and growth, and reflecting a dedication to perfection that transcends size.Today, Bocaveli stands as a symbol of quality and luxury design in Iran. We don’t just build cabinetry; we create spaces that invite you to experience a luxurious and enduring way of living.

ABOUT & HISTORY

Bocaveli began as a small design studio focused on bespoke cabinetry and interior solutions. Over two decades, it evolved into a leading brand recognized for its architectural approach to kitchens and living spaces. Partnering with European brands, Bocaveli emphasizes quality, creativity, and functionality, redefining luxury through timeless craftsmanship and innovative designs.
